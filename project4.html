HR Employee Attrition Dashboard

Situation: An organization was facing high employee turnover, leading to increased costs and loss of knowledge, but lacked clear insights into the root causes.

Task: My goal was to analyze employee data to identify factors driving attrition and create a Power BI dashboard for the HR department to monitor trends and pinpoint high-risk employee segments.

Action: I used SQL to clean and prepare the HR data. Then, I built an interactive dashboard in Power BI with KPIs like attrition rate and average salary. The dashboard included drill-downs by department, job role, and demographics to explore patterns.

Result: The dashboard transformed raw HR data into an actionable tool. It enabled the HR team to shift from reactive to proactive, identifying high-turnover departments and creating a data-backed foundation for targeted retention strategies, such as revising compensation for at-risk roles.
