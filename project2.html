BankSphere: Retail Banking Analytics

Situation: A retail bank had vast, siloed data on 25K customers but lacked a unified view of customer behavior, profitability, and risk.

Task: I was tasked with developing "BankSphere," an end-to-end analytics solution, culminating in an 8-page Power BI dashboard to provide a holistic view of portfolio health.

Action: I performed data cleaning and analysis using SQL and Python. In Power BI, I authored over 20 advanced DAX measures from scratch to calculate critical KPIs like Customer Lifetime Value (CLV), default risk, and profitability margins.

Result: The dashboard identified ₹8B in potential cross-sell opportunities among high-value customers. This provided a clear, data-backed roadmap for launching targeted marketing campaigns to directly drive revenue growth and enhance portfolio profitability.
