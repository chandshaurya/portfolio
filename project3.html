NYC Yellow Taxi Analytics

Situation: The NYC Taxi Commission's massive dataset of trips was difficult to analyze at a high level, hindering strategic planning and operational oversight.

Task: My objective was to analyze a dataset of 20M+ taxi trips and engineer an interactive Power BI dashboard to distill this information into actionable insights.

Action: I modeled the large dataset in Power BI and used advanced DAX to engineer KPIs, monitoring $516M in revenue and 38.68% growth. I developed a dashboard with time-series charts, revenue breakdowns, and geospatial heatmaps to visualize high-demand pickup and drop-off locations.

Result: The dashboard successfully transformed 20 million raw data points into an insightful analytical tool. It provided stakeholders a powerful lens to monitor financial health and operational trends, with heatmaps offering clear insights for optimizing fleet management.
