MarketPulse 360: E-Commerce Analytics

Situation: An e-commerce company needed to make data-driven decisions regarding pricing and marketing but lacked consolidated insights from their vast sales and customer data.

Task: My goal was to develop a solution to analyze over 1M sales records and 40K reviews, delivering a dynamic Power BI dashboard to visualize revenue, customer sentiment, and competitor pricing gaps.

Action: I wrote 43+ complex SQL queries for data transformation, performed in-depth EDA in Python, and then designed an interactive Power BI dashboard. The dashboard visualized KPIs like ₹30B in revenue, 65% positive sentiment, and a 0.06% return rate, with drill-down capabilities for granular analysis.

Result: The dashboard provided actionable insights that empowered the business to strengthen its competitive positioning. The customer cohort analysis enabled the development of targeted marketing campaigns to improve loyalty and drive growth.
